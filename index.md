## Privacy Policy (English)

GreenCardFlow ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

GreenCardFlow does **not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data**.

GreenCardFlow does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

### 4. Data Security
Since no personal data is collected, no personal information is stored or processed.

本应用：
